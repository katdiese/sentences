How Sentences Works

The objective of Setences is to create as many sentences as you can before time is up.

  • You will be provided with a wordbank of scrambled words -- you will need all of these!
  • Once the game begins, drag and drop the words in containers in the correct order to build the sentence.
  • When your sentence is complete, press NEXT to move to the next sentence
  • Easy Mode will tell you when you have put the word in the right place
  • Hard Mode will NOT tell you when you have placed words correctly
  • Insanity mode is not for the faint of heart!
  • POINTS SYSTEM: if you complete the whole sentence correctly, you will receive a point for every word in the sentence. If not, no points!

Good luck!
